Mirabai Applegate

Mirabai Applegate is a lover of adventure and challenge - based in San Francisco, California.
With a passion for organized and logical systems, Mirabai tackles hard problems with enthusiasm and a desire for personal growth. While hiking the Pacific Crest Trail last year she spent countless hours pondering what the next great challenge of her life should be. With a background in Business Management she wanted practical development skills that would allow the agile creation of ideas that make the world a more easy, understandable and pleasurable place.
Hackbright Academy's Engineering Fellowship was the next logical step when paired with her life long excitement about technology. Now that she has successfully completed the program she's prepared for the next challenge, her career as a software engineer.
